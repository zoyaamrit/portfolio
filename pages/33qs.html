33 Answers to Fall in Love captures the portrait and voice of an individual through their responses to a series of interview questions. The interview is adopted from a study that aims to accelerate intimacy between strangers through 36 increasingly personal questions.

The book uses expressive text and typography to convey the voice of the interviewee, aided by drawn and captured images interspersed through the text. This was showcased at Arts Collaborative Medford.
