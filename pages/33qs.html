33 Answers to Fall in Love captures the portrait an individual by representing their responses to an interview. The book uses expressive text and typography to convey the voice of the interviewee, aided by drawn and captured images interspersed through the text. The interview is adopted from a study that aims to accelerate intimacy between strangers through 36 increasingly personal questions.

This was showcased at Arts Collaborative Medford and is on sale at the SMFA art sale.
