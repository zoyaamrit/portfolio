Circle of life is a project on Processing that uses basic visual properties of circles and lines to demonstrate the complexity of interpersonal relationships. It is specifically focused towards human growth and the supportive role of the community in accomodating development. It is also a long-term piece as someone would have to observe the system over a period of time to notice the evolution. It is an interactive piece and will eventually be on some public forum, but can be seen on github for now. Scroll down for images.
