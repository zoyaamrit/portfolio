Circle of life is a project on Processing that uses basic visual properties of circles and lines to demonstrate the complexity of interpersonal relationships. It is specifically focused towards human growth and the supportive role of the community in accomodating development. Although an inherently interactive piece, it also demands patience as a user would have to observe the system over period of time to notice its evolution. While not currently hosted on a website, you can view it at github for now. Scroll down for images.
